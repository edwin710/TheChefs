Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 35 minutes.

Instructions:
1. Sautee onions and garlic on medium heat. Chop broccoli and carrots.
2. Add broth, broccoli, and carrots, and boil on medium heat for 10 minutes.
3. Combine milk and flour, stir with whisk until blended. Add mixture to soup and cook for 5 minutes.
4. Coat squash in butter and 1 tsp of brown sugar.
5. Add cheese, stir until combine, and serve.
