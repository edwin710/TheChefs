Carrot Hummus

Prep Time: 25 minutes. Cook Time: 0 minutes.

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: carrots, garlic, tahini, lemon

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

2. Roast carrots until tender

3. In a food processor, combine the roasted carrots and garlic with the tahini, cilantro, lemon juice and 5 tablespoons water. Pulse until smooth. With the processor running, slowly add 2 to 3 tablespoons olive oil.

4. Transfer to serving bowl
