Baked Acorn Squash

Prep Time: 0 minutes. Cook Time: 90 minutes.

Main Ingredients: acorn squash, butter, brown sugar

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheight.
2. Wrap with oiled foil and bake squash for 1 hour, or until soft.
3. Unwrap squash and cut in half, then scoop out seeds.
4. Coat squash in butter and 1 tsp of brown sugar.
5. Bake for another 20 minutes.
