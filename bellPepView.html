Vegetarian Fajitas

Prep Time: 15 minutes. Cook Time: 20 minutes.

Total Time: 35 minutes.

Main Ingredients: bell pepper, mushrooms, onion, lime, paprika, cilantro

Instructions:

1. Preheat a 12 inch cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add in oil, onions, and peppers. Saute for 4-5 minutes or until onions begin to soften and caramelize.

2. Add in garlic, portabello mushrooms and spices. Saute for 4-5 minutes longer until mushrooms soften

3. Turn off the heat and add in the zest and juice of one lime.
