Sweet Potato Chili

Prep Time: 30 minutes. Cook Time: 75 minutes.

Total Time: 105 minutes

Ingredients: sweet potato, onion, veggie stock, black beans, salsa

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, sweat onions. Stir and continue cooking till translucent and soft.

2. Add sweet potato and any desired spices. Cook for 3 minutes. Add salsa, water, and veggie stock.

3. Bring mixture to a boil then lower heat to simmer. Add black beans. Cook till sweet potato is tender.

4. Serve with cilantro, onion, avocado and/or lime juice.
