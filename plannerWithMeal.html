Pumpkin Oatmeal

Prep Time: 2 mins Cook Time: 8 mins Total Time: 10 mins
Servings: 4 servings Calories: 540 calories


Ingredients
  • 1 16-ounce pumpkin puree
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/8 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 2 cups oats
  • 10 ounces evaporated milk
Instructions
  • Mix together oats and milk in a microwave-safe bowl.
  • Cook on high for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring once.
  • Add more milk or oats to achieve the desired consistency, and cook for another 30 seconds.
  • Stir in pumpkin puree, pumpkin pie spice, and cinnamon sugar.
  • Heat through, and serve.
